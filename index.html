Belarus: A Discovery in the Heart of Europe

A beautiful country you need to see.

Where is Belarus?

Belarus is a welcoming green country right in the center of Europe. It's a peaceful land of beautiful forests and quiet rivers, sharing borders with five other nations. This central location gives it a unique blend of cultures and landscapes.

5

Neighboring Countries

  • Poland
  • Ukraine
  • Russia
  • Lithuania
  • Latvia

Land of Blue Lakes & Green Forests

Often called the "lungs of Europe," Belarus is blessed with incredible nature. Its vast, ancient forests and thousands of clear lakes create a stunning landscape. It's also a sanctuary for rare wildlife, including the majestic European bison.

Home of the European Bison

One of the last places on Earth to see this big and rare animal in its natural habitat, the ancient Belovezhskaya Pushcha forest. In Belarus, we call him "Zubr," and he is a mighty hero of many national legends.

A Land of Water and Woods

With over 11,000 lakes and vast forests covering about 40% of the country, nature is always close by.

Travel to the Past: Old Castles

Belarus has a rich and deep history waiting to be explored. You can visit amazing old castles that look like they are from a fairy tale. Two of its most famous castles are so important they are protected as UNESCO World Heritage sites.

Mir Castle

A stunning example of 16th-century fortification art, blending Gothic, Baroque, and Renaissance architecture.

Nesvizh Palace

An incredible residential castle of the Radziwill family, surrounded by beautiful parks and lakes.

Talented People, Tasty Food

The heart of Belarus is its friendly and hospitable people. They are known for their beautiful traditional crafts and, of course, their delicious national food. A visit isn't complete without trying the famous "draniki"!

Beautiful Crafts

Belarusians are masters of making beautiful and intricate items from natural materials like linen and straw.

Delicious Draniki

You must try the national dish, "draniki." They are wonderfully tasty potato pancakes, often served with sour cream.

Welcome to Belarus!

From the clean, modern streets of its capital city, Minsk, to the quiet, untouched nature, Belarus is a country of wonderful surprises. Thank you for your attention!
